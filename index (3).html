Our Story

Poured with Care Since Day One

Uncle Ratt began as a single shelf of well-chosen bottles and a promise: never sell anything we wouldn't pour ourselves. Today that promise still stands across whiskey, wine, gin, cognac and everything between.

We work directly with trusted distributors to keep every bottle authentic, every price fair, and every order moving fast — from our counter to your glass.

  • 100% authentic, sealed bottles
  • Temperature-controlled storage
  • Same-day dispatch on local orders
  • Age verification on every sale
